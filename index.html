About Me

I am a UI/UX enthusiastic, currently working at United Shore, Troy Michigan USA. My Job include the following

  • Follow guidelines and standards to enforce consistency throughout all of our products
  • Meet with our customers and product owners to understand the problems they face, and propose alternatives to create the ideal user experience to meet the business need
  • Fec in HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript
  • Help other developers trouble-shoot UI issues
  • Creating beautiful and usable web applications that meet our branding and coding standards
  • Advise and educate other developers on best practices
  • Research new technologies and methods to continuously improve our applications
